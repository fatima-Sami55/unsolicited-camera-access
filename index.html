Your Camera Was Accessed Without Consent

This page silently accessed your webcam and recorded a short video clip, then saved it to your browser's local storage all without asking for permission.

VULNERABILITY: Unsolicited getUserMedia Access

Root Cause: Browsers grant camera access to any origin with existing permission. Malicious scripts can exploit cached grants to silently record users across sessions.If you see your camera LED light up on a page that shouldn't need your camera close it immediately.

In a real attack, this footage could be stored on a remote server, used for blackmail, identity verification bypass, or surveillance. This demonstration proves the consent gap in browser permission models.
